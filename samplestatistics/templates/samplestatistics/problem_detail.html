Here is the formula you can use to calculate the sample mean.

$$ \bar{x} = \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i $$

In this formula, there are several symbols to know:

$$ \bar{x} $$

the sample mean

$$ n $$

the sample size

$$ x_i $$

the ith data value

$$ \sum $$

the act of summing the expression to its right

Show Solution

Hide Solution

$$ \begin{align} \bar{x} &= \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i \\ &= \frac{1}{4} \sum_{i=1}^{4} x_i \\ &= \frac{1}{4} \left( 52 + 1 + 89 + 52 \right) \\ &= \frac{1}{4} \left( 194 \right) \\[1ex] &= 48.5 \\ \end{align} $$

Thus, the mean of these 4 values is 48.5.
